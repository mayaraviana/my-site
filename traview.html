WHAT

Traview is an application where it is possible to solve various processes needed before traveling to a defined destination in a fast and centralized way.

WHY

People who travel end up having a lot work and entering different platforms to buy tickets, rent places, see reviews, buy tickets for tours, etc. This process can be time consuming and many times it does not contain real photos of the place.

THE PROBLEM

People who travel end up having a lot work and entering different platforms to buy tickets, rent places, see reviews, buy tickets for tours, etc. This process can be time consuming and many times it does not contain real photos of the place.

RESEARCH

How do users plan today when deciding to travel?

Search about different locations on google to decide a destination.

One of the user's first steps is search for different destinations, starting with the ones they've already had some prior interest.

Search for places to stay, main points tourism and about locomotion.

Sites like Airbnb and Google itself are used to find accommodation and understand the region.

Search for places where can take good pictures.

In the age of the Internet, places "Instagrammables" has great influence for the user's final decision.

GOALS

How we show the possible destinations, improve the experience of user and let him have access to more real reviews?

SOLUTION
