WORKING ON

A website redesign for Boden Club

Boden Club is an online coffee subscription company. We are developing a simpler and more interesting site for its target audience: older people and who are not so comfortable with technology.

The initial plan was:

1. Mapping of all current Boden website (main features and pages).
2. User testing to understand current site issues.
3. Search for references in the same market.
4. Development of low-fidelity prototypes. [current step]
5. User tests.
6. Refinement of prototypes.
7. Delivery and documentation.
